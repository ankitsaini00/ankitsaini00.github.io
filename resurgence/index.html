About Resurgence


Resurgence, the Annual Cultural festival of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University (SMVDU) , is one of the most popular college fest in the northern India. Students and performers from across the country are invited to be part of this fest, compete with one another at a common platform.

This year the fest completes 9 years of success and we promise you a Resurgence bigger and better than ever before. The sky is the limit as we turn SMVDU into a full fledged carnival brimming with energy, enthusiasm and fun. Watch your fantasies come to life at RESURGENCE 2k18.

It is one of the largest platforms for college students across the country to come together to celebrate culture, fine arts and social awareness. Resurgence is a place for the passionate, the sportive, the energetic and the enthusiastic to interact with other like minded peers. Riding on the success of previous years Resurgence comes to you this year with a theme that manifests MAGIC AND EXCITEMENT. We proudly invite you to experience the very spirit of festivity, unbridled energy and passion at Resurgence 2k18.

LITERARY EVENTS

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

Every House needs to report fifteen (15) minutes prior to the event.
Presentations/Documents of events need to be submitted two days before the event.
The name(s) of Participants of the Literary Events need(s) to be submitted two days prior to the event.

  1. Hindi Debate


    2. Team Size:   2 from each House, one for the motion & the other against the motion.
    Time limit:  Each member will be given 4 minutes to speak and 1 minute to refute. The refutation shall begin with the last speaker. 1 st warning Buzzer will be after 3 minutes.
    Topic:   Issue for the Debate will be decided by the coordinators of the event.
    1. Medium of presentation is Hindi only.
    2. Paper reading is not allowed. One may however refer to noted points.
    Judging Criteria:   Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content, suitability and propriety of language
    Matter – the logical reasoning and content presented by the debater Manner – presentation and delivery, the style of speaking, body language. Method – structure and organization of the presentation made by the speakers.

  2. English Debate


    3. Team Size:   2 from each House, one for the motion & the other against the motion.
    Time limit:  Each member will be given 4 minutes to speak and 1 minute to refute. The refutation shall begin with the last speaker. 1 st warning Buzzer will be after 3 minutes.
    Topic:   Issue for the Debate will be decided by the Faculty Coordinators of the event.
    1. Medium of presentation is English only.
    2. Paper reading is not allowed. One may however refer to noted points.
    Judging Criteria:   Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content, suitability and propriety of language
    Matter – the logical reasoning and content presented by the debater Manner – presentation and delivery, the style of speaking, body language. Method – structure and organization of the presentation made by the speakers.

  3. Poetry


    4. Team Size:   3 (1 for English, 1 for Hindi and 1 for Urdu) from each House
    Time limit:  3-5 minutes for each recitation
    Topic:   Topic will be given only two hours before the event and the participant is required to write poetry in a place specified by the event coordinators.
    1. Medium of poetry is English, Hindi and Urdu.
    2. The participant will have to submit the content of the poem in writing to the judges after the recitation.
    Judging Criteria:   Choice of words, diction, creativity, and style of recitation, relevance to the theme.

  4. Creative Writing


    5. Team Size:   2 from each House.
    Time limit:  2 hours.
    Topic:   1. The situation/theme and first line of the story will be announced on the spot.
    2. The participants are required to make a well-formed, original story. The
    situation/theme may belong to any time, any era, any place, and any culture. The level of expression must appropriate the character of the situation/theme.
    3. The story should be of 600 – 1200 words.
    4. The language of writing can be English/Hindi.
    Judging Criteria:   1. Ability to captivate the reader till the end
    2. Originality and articulation.
Coordinators:-  Shivangi Jandiya & Manglam Mishra.

MUSIC EVENTS

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

All Songs will be submitted before April 03, 2018. In case of same songs, first come first serve basis will be followed.
The need of any instruments (Mic's, Tables, Chair, etc.) should be informed earlier before March 04, 2018.
The name of Participants of the Music Events need to be submitted before March 04, 2018.

  1. Light Indian Vocal


    2. Team Size:   1 participants from each house + 5 accompanists (max).
    Dress Code:   Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
    Time limit:  3-5 minutes for performance + 3 minutes for set up and clearance
    Instruments Provided:   2 Guitars, 1 Bass, 1 Drum Kit, 1 Synthesizer and 1 Tabla-set
    1. The participants are required to come prepared with a song of their choice to showcase their versatility.
    2. Ghazal/bhajan/shabad/Sufi and Abhangas can be presented.
    3. Karaoke not allowed.
    4. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
    Judging Criteria:   VJudgment is on the qualities like Swara, Taal, Innovation, Synchronization, Selection of Raga, Composition and Overall Impression.

  2. Sangam


    3. Team Size:   5-12 partcipants including the accompanist.(min 4 vocalist is compulsory in a team)
    Time limit:  6-9 minutes (including 2 min for setup and clearance time)
    Rules:   1. A team member can sing and play an instrument as well. However such a person would be counted as both vocalist and accompanist. Teams may use 15 minimum zero to maximum two instrumentalists.
    2. Only Piano mode in key board is allowed, touch sense is allowed in keyboard.
    3. Time Limit should be strictly followed. Participants will be penalized by judges for not doing so.
    4. The decision of the organizers with respect to line-up would be final.
    5. The decision of the judges will be binding.
    6. The team may sing more than one song within the time limit. 7. Karaoke not allowed.
    7.Accompanist from other houses are allowed.
    Judging Criteria:   Content, Rhythm, Harmonies, Delivery and quality of Vocals, Presentation, Synchronization, Stage presence and overall performance as a group.

  3. Western Vocal


    4. Team Size:  1-2 from each House +5 accompanists (max).
    Dress Code:  Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
    Time limit:  3-5 minutes for performance + 3 minutes for set up and clearance Instruments Provided: 2 Guitars, 1 Bass Guitar, 1 Drum Kit and 1 Synthesizer.
    1. Only Western songs with no classical tinge are allowed.
    2. Genres can be anything ranging from Rock to Rap and from Death Metal to Soft Singing.
    3. Language of the song can be anything excluding those of Indian Subcontinents.
    4. Karaoke not allowed.
    5. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
    Judging Criteria:  Judgment is made on the qualities like rhythm, vocals, composition coordination and overall impression.

  4. Hindi Duet


    5. Team Size:  2 from each house + 5 accompanists (max).
    Dress Code:  Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
    Time Limit:  5 minutes for performance + 2 minutes for set up and clearance.
    Instruments Provided:  2 Guitars, 1 Bass, 1 Drum Kit, 1 Synthesizer and 1 Tabla-set
    1. The participants have to come prepared with a song of their choice to showcase their versatility.
    2. The songs must have Hindi lyrics.
    3. Karaoke not allowed.
    4. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
    Judging Criteria:  Synchronization, Presentation, Rhythm, delivery and quality of vocals and overall impact.

  5. Battle Of Bands


    6. Team Size:  4-8 participants.
    Time Limit:  5-8 minutes for performance + 5 minutes for set up and clearance.
    Instruments provided:-  one drum set, one table, one synthesizer) Rules:-
    1. At least 1 vocalist and 3 instruments required, including 1 percussion instrument compulsory.(Max 4 instrumentalist from other houses are allowed)
    2. Songs should only be in Hindi (no other languages allowed).So, It will be a Hindi Rock Band.
    3. Rap refrains are not allowed.
    4. Purely classical numbers are not allowed, however a classical refrain is permitted.
    5. Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
    6. The time limit should strictly be followed. Participants will be penalized by judges for not doing so.
    7. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
    8. The decision of the organizers regarding the final line-up would be final.
    Judging Criteria:  Quality of vocals, quality of instrumental music, synchronization, overall impact as a band.

  6. Qawwali


    7. Team Size:  Main singers: 1-2 Accompanists: 4-6
    Time limit:  5-8 minutes (3 minutes set up and clearance time) 19
    Dress Code:  Salwar Suit/Kurta-Pyjama (optional), Topi and Jacket (optional)
    Instruments Provided:  Harmonium, Tabla and Synthesizer,
    1. The participants are required to perform a prepared Qawwali, which is a form of Sufi devotional music. The performance has to be primarily in Hindi (although light usage of Urdu is acceptable)
    2. Usage of any other instrument should be notified to the coordinators and has to be arranged by the University itself.
    3. If any background music or beat is used, it would lead to negative marking or even disqualification.
    4. No professional accompanists are allowed.
    Judging Criteria:   Content, Delivery and quality of Vocals, Presentation, Synchronization, and overall impact.
Coordinators:-  Paras Dubey & Shubham Upadhyay.

FINE ARTS EVENTS

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

Every House need to report fifteen (15) minutes before event get started.
Colors of the Cloth for Banner of the House need to be decided on the meeting.

  1. Rangoli


    2. Team size:  Maximum of 4 from each house
    Time limit:  2 hrs
    Materials:  Free to use any traditional Rangoli material (usage of the colors which deface the floor is not allowed)
    Area provided:  1.2 m x 1.2 m The participants will be given a space of 1.2mx1.2m in which they have to depict the topic given to them in the stipulated time. The participants will have to submit their version of topic in not more than 100 words to the event coordinator.
    Judging Criteria:  Concept, Color composition, Relevance to the theme, Neatness, Finesse

  2. Face Painting


    3. Team size:  2 participant from each house
    Time limit:  2 hours
    Materials:      Any color medium of your choice Participant will be given a topic one hour before the event. The output should be in relevance with the topic given.
    Judging Criteria:  Concept, Overall effect

  3. Model Making


    4. Team Size:  2(individuals) from each house
    Time:  2 hrs
    Material:  two ivory will be provided, but you need to get cutter, glue and other drafting material of your own. Topic will be given on the spot.
    Judging Criteria:  Perfection, Cleanliness of Model, and Closeness to the given topic.
    Further innovation on the topic would be helped by Fine Arts Coordinator.

  4. Stippling


    5. Team Size:  2 from each house
    Time Limit:  2 hours
    Materials:  Rotring Pen, Black ink only allowed (NOT PROVIDED BY COORDINATING TEAM)
    Judging Criteria:  Creativity, Overall Effect

  5. Graffiti


    6. Team Size:  3 participants
    Time duration:  4 hours.
    Materials:  Materials shall be provided (spray cans, mountboard, ivory sheets, cutters, pencils, markers). Participants are allowed to carry their own materials, if any. Size:      The size of the graffiti shall be 5’ X 4’.
    Judging Criteria:  Creativity, Overall Effect
Coordinators:-  Paras Dubey & Shubham Upadhyay.

THEATRE EVENTS

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

The need of any Props should be informed earlier before March 30, 2018.
Story/Theme of all the events need to be submitted before March 30, 2018. In case of same story first come first serve basis will be allowed.

  1. Street Play (Nukkad Natak)


    2. Team size:  Upto 12 participants from each House.
    Time Limit:  10 - 15 minutes.
    1. Medium of the performance is Hindi.
    2. The play should contain a social message.
    3. Musical instruments using electric power are not allowed. Any other Musical instruments, including unconventional ones are allowed and will be considered as props.
    4. No pre-recorded music or voice is allowed.
    5. No microphones will be provided.
    Judging Criteria:  Creative interpretation of theme and Impact of theme, Group co-ordination, Presentation, Contact with & involvement of the Audience and Overall impact.

  2. Skit


    3. Team Size:  Upto 8 participants
    Time limit:  10-12 minutes (includes setup and clearance time)
    1. Each presentation should have a title and a theme.
    2. Medium of performing the Skit should be Hindi.
    3. The Skit can involve multiple scenes and blackouts.
    4. Backstage voice overs or sounds or narration are allowed during the Performance.
    5. Background or Pre-recorded music is allowed.
    Production material provided:  1 table, 2 chairs.
    Any other production material has to be brought by the teams but the Organizers reserve the right to allow its use on the stage.
    Judging Criteria:  Presentation of the theme, Group co-ordination, Dialogue Delivery and Overall impact, Acting, Make-up and Costumes according to the Act.

  3. Stage Drama


    4. Team size:  Upto 15 (participants + Accompanists).
    Time Limit:  25-35 minutes (including stage setup and clearance time).
    1. Live music and backstage voices are allowed.
    2. The play should be in Hindi language whereas small portions of other languages may be used
    3. If using background music it should be made sure that there are no dialogues present in the music or negative marks accordingly will be rewarded.
    4. Recorded sounds are not allowed during the Performance.
    Production material provided:  1 table, 2 chairs Any other production material has to be brought by the teams but the Organizers reserve the right to allow its use on the stage.
    Judging Criteria:  Music, expressions, body language, overall impact, make-up and costumes according to the Act, stage setup, and synchronization with dialogues.

  4. Mime


    5. Team Size: bsp; Upto 10 participants(max)
    Time limit:  5-10 minutes (includes setup and clearance time)
    1. The act should not contain any dialogues, lip sync or props.
    2. No act shall contain any offensive, obscene, disrespectful actions or gestures. The act will be immediately stopped and the entry will be disqualified, if these instructions are not followed
    3. Only recorded music can be used, during the enactment, no lyrics please.
    4. The act should be able to convey a message.
    Judging Criteria:  Points will be given on the basis of innovation, depiction of the situation, team work and expressions
Coordinators:-  Bhavia Sharma & Shubham Upadhyay.

SPECIAL EVENTS

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

The name of Participants of the Special Events need to be submitted before two days of events.

  1. Fashion Show


    2. Team Size:  10 from each House, including 5 models and 1 main designer, 1 make-up designer, 1 hair designer and 2 assistant designer.
    Rules: 
    Each house has to prepare main design using dark colors according to the theme. Material may include products made from paper, plastics, rubber, etc. Use of Paper is allowed for making dresses but fabric should be used as the base and length of dress should be up to knee length and protective layers should be used as per order by the Dean of students.
    Judging Criteria:
    - Use of the colors
    - Original and creative approach.
    - Overall Effect.
    Theme:
    Will be discussed with the committee and all the house coordinator.

  2. Mea Culpa


    3. Team Size:  3 members team from each house, Including 1 prosecutor (M/F)
    Time limit: 
    5-8 minutes for each defender Topic:  
    Characters will be assigned to both the members (1M+1F) of a House 3 days before the event through a raffle (chit system).
    The prosecutor will coordinate the proceeding enacting the role of a trial taker. Every House will pose only one question to each defender of the other House. For this purpose, a House will prepare 8 allegations, 2 each for the defenders of every other House. Moreover, a House is required to submit the names of four students, one for every other House, who will ask the questions. In the last, the prosecutor can entertain at most one open question from the audience. Judges can interfere to discard any questions from the audience, if necessary. Judging Criteria:
    Defender
    1. Argumentative value of the defence
    2. Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content, suitability and propriety of language
    Prosecutor
    1. Effective coordination of the proceeding
    2. Presence of Mind
    3. Critical Attitude

  3. Youth Parliament


    4. As per National Youth Parliament Competition Scheme for Universities/Colleges by Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs Government of India.


    Coordinators:-  Paras Dubey & Shubham Upadhyay.
